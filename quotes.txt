Motivational Quotes

Invest in Yourself
Don't be content with just what you have today and tomorrow. 
Think what you need to possess to weather all storms of your life and still come out smiling and loaded for generations. 
Motivational speak and all are excellent but in concrete, don't leave your destiny in someone's else hands. 
You can respect mentors and experts but take pride in the fact that when you do dare take the risk, you are sole master of 
your destiny and you carve out your own path.
